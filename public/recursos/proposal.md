# RuruLab Website Redesign

Version: 1.0

---

# Project Overview

## Vision

Transform RuruLab's website into the leading digital platform for coffee, cacao and honey producers, cooperatives, roasters, chocolatiers and exporters seeking traceability, commercialization and product digitalization solutions.

The current website primarily communicates QR traceability functionality, while the platform offers a significantly broader ecosystem including:

* Product Digital Passports
* QR Traceability
* Sensory Management
* Marketplace B2B
* Certifications
* EUDR Compliance
* Geolocation Services
* Digital Producer Profiles
* QR Menus
* Commercialization Tools

The redesigned website must position RuruLab as:

> The Digitalization Platform for Coffee, Cacao and Honey Value Chains.
